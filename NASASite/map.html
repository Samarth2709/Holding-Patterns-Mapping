Interactive Map

Our interactive map shows the levels of carbon emissions in each state. There is also a section that shows the carbon emissions per capita, so that people can better understand the context as to why some states have higher levels. People can also switch the year that they are viewing on the map in order to see how the levels of carbon emissions have changed over the years. This map was coded using the Python library Plotly-Dash, and the data came from the EIA (Energy Information Administration).
